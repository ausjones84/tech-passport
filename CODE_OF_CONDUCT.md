# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Tech Passport is a platform built to help engineers from every background, country, and experience level break into cloud careers. We are committed to making this community welcoming, inclusive, and supportive for everyone.

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Encouraged behavior:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of different viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Sharing knowledge and helping others learn

### Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Gatekeeping — telling others they are "not good enough" or "don't belong"
- Any conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting us through [techpassportapp.com](https://techpassportapp.com). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
